Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DEVCONF 2026.

When is DEVCONF 2026?

DEVCONF 2026 will be held from August 15–17, 2026, featuring three days of talks, workshops, and networking.

Where is the event?

The conference will take place at the International Convention Center with both in-person and online participation.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. Every attendee will receive access to all recorded sessions for 30 days after the conference.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Tickets are refundable up to 14 days before the event.

Are meals included?

Lunch, snacks, and coffee breaks are included with every ticket.

How can I contact the organizers?

Contact us anytime at support@devconf2026.com.
